Data Scientist and Software Developer

Welcome to my site. I’m a Data Scientist who started as a Software Developer in Microsoft. I started programming from very young age. My early programs are in C++ for DOS and in VB6 for Windows 9x. My early projects on DOS includes my own graphics library for VESA SVGA, a raytracing engine, ‘wav’ music player with visualizations, GUI library and Checkers game with AI.

As I started doing min-projects of my own from 2003, I’ve worked on many languages and technologies. I’ve a proferssional experience in C++, C#, Java, Python and Javascript. However, I’ve also worked on C, Basic, Assembly(x86), Matlab and ActionScript for some of my own projects. Computer graphics and AI are my favourite fields. Sometimes I code on hackerrank and projecteuler.

Apart from computers and maths my hobbies are drawing, cycling, reading, gaming and watching IMDB top rated movies, TV shows and anime. I enjoy music of almost all the genres. Apart from that I’m interested in painting, playing music and learning new things.
